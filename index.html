Research

I am currently NLP Research Scientist and Manager at QWANT.

My field of research is multilingual Natural Language Processing jointly with Deep Learning for Information Retrieval.

I focus on modeling context for several fields of NLP: semantic content analysis and modeling, machine translation, Intent Detection in real-life application at scale.

Biography

NLP Research Scientist and Manager at QWANT since 2017. Deputy director of the INRIA-QWANT Lab since 2018.

Previously, he was applied researcher at SYSRAN to work on domain adaptation for NMT (2016-2017), member of the GETALP team at the Univesity of Grenoble to develop Deep Learning approaches for SMT evaluation (2015-2016), part of the Xerox Research Centre Europe (XRCE) in the Machine Learning for Document Access and Translation (MLDAT) group (2013-2015), postdoc researcher in the Speech and Language Technology group at the laboratory of the University of Le Mans, France (LIUM), research engineer at the Vision & Content Engineering Laboratory at the CEA LIST (2009-2010).

He received a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Avignon, France in 2008.
